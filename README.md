📊 Predictive Retail Sales Analytics & Demand Forecasting Dashboard
🚀 Project Overview
Predictive Retail Sales Analytics & Demand Forecasting Dashboard is an
end-to-end retail analytics project built using Python, Machine
Learning, Time Series Forecasting, and Streamlit.
The project analyzes retail transaction data to understand sales trends,
product performance, customer behavior, inventory efficiency, and
promotion impact. It also predicts future demand using forecasting
models to support better business decisions.
---
🎯 Objectives
Analyze historical retail sales data
Identify sales trends and patterns
Perform product performance analysis
Understand customer purchasing behavior
Monitor inventory and stockout risks
Evaluate promotion effectiveness
Forecast future demand
Generate business recommendations
